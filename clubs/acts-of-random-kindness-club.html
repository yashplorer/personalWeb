Acts of Random Kindness

We high school students face stress, anxiety, drama, and negativity from time to time. The Acts of Random Kindness Club strives to make high school a little more pleasant by conducting uplifting campaigns, activities, and projects, because it's the little things in life that make a difference.

After meeting with Sabrina Ma (founder of ARK), I thought I could really change my school for the better by bringing the Acts of Random Kindness club. Sabrina spoke at San Mateo in April and somewhat established our member base with the attendees. I've added links to like the Facebook Page for SMHS ARK to the left, and have started an official group here.

I'm really looking forward to serving as President of ARK at San Mateo and really feel that it'll enable me to connect members of the community that I typically fail to see at most of the clubs that I lead.
