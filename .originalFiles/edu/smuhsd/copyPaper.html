Copy Paper Amendment

Looking through the district budget's estimated expenditures (should be avalible here, but I had to call in and ask them to email it to me) toward materials and supplies and noticing amount of paper used daily by students and their teachers (I'll soon have photos up of the interesting applications of the millions we put toward our schools' paper supply here), I realized that we would probably save money and conserve more resources if we were to use partially recycled paper. I'm in contact with those in charge of paper purchases at my high school and am currently pursuing alternatives to our current paper.
